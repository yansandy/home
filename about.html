About my friends

General

Youthful

I genuinely love and adore all of my friends for more than just who they are, but who they were before and who they will be in the future. I believe that every single one of them will do amazing things and I'm always so proud of them, no matter the outcome. They are some of the most hardworking, kind, and funny people I know. I hope that every single one of them makes their dreams come true because I truly believe that they deserve the best in life. I would not change the way that things are now for anything in the world. Every single one of them has had such an impact on who I am and how I act every day in my life. They've all shaped the person that I am today and I could never express how thankful I am to have them in my life every day. I could not imagine a reality without them, even if I wanted to. I hope that they all continue to grow and thrive. You too. I hope that anyone who reads this grows and thrives and becomes a wonderful person, not by my standards, but someone that you can be proud of.

Youthful is a name that we coined for a groupchat a few years back, near the beginning of the pandemic. Ever since, I've grown so unbelievably close to all of them and I don't think I'll ever be able to replace them. Every single one of them holds a special place in my heart and I hope that I can see them grow and thrive into the beautiful people that I know for a fact they will become. I truly hope that every single one of them finds what they're looking for in life and if not, I'll still be there with them for the entire journey.
